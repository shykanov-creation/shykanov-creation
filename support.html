🛠 Support

Need help with one of our games? We're happy to assist — here's how to reach us.

How to get support

The best way to reach us is by email at [SUPPORT_EMAIL]. We try to respond to every message, though it may take a little time as we're a small team.

We can help with things like:

  • 🐞 Bug reports — something not working as expected
  • 🎮 Gameplay issues — trouble with levels, controls, or progress
  • 👤 Account & data questions — questions about your saved data or account
  • 💬 Feedback — ideas, suggestions, or general thoughts on our games

Before you contact us

To help us help you faster, please include the following details when you write to us:

  • The name of the game or app
  • A short description of the issue
  • Your device model and Android version
  • The app version (found in the app's settings or info screen, if available)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible

What to expect

We aim to read and respond to support messages as quickly as we can. Response times may vary depending on the volume of requests and the complexity of the issue.
