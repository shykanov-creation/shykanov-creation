🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

This Privacy Policy explains how Shykanov Creation ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our apps and this website. This is a general template that applies across our apps; where a specific app collects information differently, that app's store listing or in-app notice will provide additional details.

Note for editors: this page is written to be easy to adapt for each individual app. Update the sections below to reflect exactly what each app does before publishing it.

Information we collect

Some of our apps may not collect any personal information at all. Where an app does collect information, it may include things such as:

  • Information you choose to provide directly, such as messages you send to support
  • Basic technical information needed for the app to function, such as device type or app version
  • Gameplay data stored locally on your device, such as progress or settings

We do not knowingly collect more information than is reasonably necessary for the app to work and for us to provide support.

How information is used

Where information is collected, it may be used to:

  • Make the app function correctly, such as saving your progress
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Understand and fix technical problems
  • Improve our apps over time

We do not sell personal information.

Third-party services

Our apps may use third-party services (for example, services provided by Google for distribution, crash reporting, or basic analytics) to help us operate and improve them. These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

Where an app uses a specific third-party service, that app's store listing or an in-app notice will identify the service and link to its privacy policy.

Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary for an app to function, and only where permitted. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information in a way that shouldn't have happened, please contact us using the details below so we can address it.

Data retention

We keep information only for as long as it's needed for the purposes described in this policy, such as providing the app's functionality or responding to a support request. Where data is stored locally on your device (for example, gameplay progress), you can typically remove it by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Your choices

Depending on the app and your device settings, you may be able to control certain types of information, such as permissions the app requests. You can also contact us at any time with questions about your information using the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in our apps or in applicable requirements. We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made.
