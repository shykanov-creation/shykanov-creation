Shykanov Creation

Shykanov Creation is an independent mobile game developer based in Norway. We create simple, fun, and accessible games for Android.

About

Shykanov Creation is a small, independent team focused on making games that are easy to pick up, enjoyable to play, and respectful of your time. We're based in Norway and build primarily for Android.

🎮 What we build

We focus on casual and arcade-style mobile games — the kind of games you can enjoy in a few spare minutes or sink into for longer sessions. Our goal is to keep things simple, polished, and fun, without unnecessary complexity or clutter.

Curious what we're working on? Visit our Games page for current and upcoming titles.

🚀 Our values

Simple & accessible

Games should be easy to start playing and welcoming to everyone, regardless of skill level.

Honest & respectful

We aim to be upfront about what our games do, what they collect, and how they work.

Quality over quantity

We'd rather release fewer games that we're proud of than rush out something half-finished.

Player-first

We listen to feedback and try to make small, steady improvements based on what players tell us.
